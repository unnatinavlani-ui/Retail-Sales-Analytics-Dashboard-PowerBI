# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze retail sales performance across products, categories, customers, cities, and months. The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions using interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large amounts of sales data, making it difficult to identify trends and monitor performance using raw data.

The objective of this project was to build an interactive Power BI dashboard that helps business users:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Identify high-value customers
- Compare sales across cities and categories
- Support data-driven business decisions

## 📈 Key Insights

- Total Sales reached **₹467.84K**.
- A total of **31 orders** were completed.
- **25 unique customers** contributed to overall sales.
- The Average Order Value was **₹15.09K**.
- Electronics generated the highest revenue among all categories.
- Laptop was the highest-selling product.
- Ahmedabad recorded the highest sales among all cities.
- August showed the highest monthly sales.
- Unnati Navlani was the top customer based on total sales.
